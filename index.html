The Snowdrift System

Unlike the one–to–one matching in traditional fundraising, Snowdrift uses a many–to–many matching pledge that creates a network effect.

How it Works:

  1. You deposit the money into your private Snowdrift.coop account.
  2. Then you pledge your monthly spport to your favorite project.
  3. You can then contribute creatively via built–in tools for discussion, tickets and requests, a wiki system, and more.
